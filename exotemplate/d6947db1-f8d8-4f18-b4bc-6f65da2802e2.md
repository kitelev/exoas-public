# $exotemplate

Homoiconic templating ontology. A `exotemplate__Template` is a vault asset whose **markdown body** is the template; it is inserted at the cursor (editor «Insert template» command, Веха 2) or copied into a newly created asset (`body_template` grounding step, Веха 3). `$token` markers in the body are resolved via the shared `exocmd__SubstitutionToken` registry (Веха 4 — reuse, no new variable class).
